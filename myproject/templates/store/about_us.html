About Us

Nepal’s Fastest-Growing Skincare & Beauty Destination

Founded in 2021, Glow & Glam began its journey with a vision to revolutionize the skincare and beauty shopping experience in Nepal. What started as a small idea to make trusted beauty products easily accessible online has now transformed into a growing beauty platform offering a curated range of skincare, makeup, and personal care essentials — all just a few clicks away.

At Glow & Glam, we believe that beauty is for everyone, and our goal is to empower individuals to feel confident and radiant in their skin, no matter where they are or what their budget is.

Our Journey

In just a few years, Glow & Glam has emerged as a trusted name in the Nepali beauty industry, connecting thousands of customers to their favorite products. We’ve expanded from offering skincare basics to becoming a go-to store for:

  • Skincare & Korean Beauty
  • Makeup
  • Haircare

Our platform is built with the latest technology, backed by a passionate Nepali team committed to creating a seamless online shopping experience.
